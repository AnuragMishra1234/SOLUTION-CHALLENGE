THE 3-GAP PROBLEM.
01
Guest → Staff Gap
Distressed guests can't reach help fast enough. Traditional phones, intercoms, and front desk calls create fatal delays in critical moments.
02
Staff → Staff Gap
No shared situational picture. Multiple staff members respond to same incident. Others miss critical ones. Radio chatter creates confusion, not clarity.
03
Staff → Responder Gap
First responders arrive blind. Verbal handoffs are lossy. Precious minutes lost explaining what trained staff already know in detail.
